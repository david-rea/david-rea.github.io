Broad Overview

My research focuses on the physics of planet formation - specifically at early times when gas turbulence plays an important role in determining whether mm-cm sized grains will grow into 100 km+ sized planetesimals

I use state-of-the-art supercomputers to run numerical simulations of planet forming disks
